900MHz

900MHz

語言、文字、符號，人類溝通的媒介，如何突破七段顯示器的框架傳遞訊息？
在一字一句都需要計價的傳訊時代，訊息內容不斷精煉演化，從數字延伸到英文字母，又或者延伸至以讀音的諧音傳達訊息，甚至當語言、文字無法表達時，改以表情符號、圖文示意。 BB Call的年代，我們以900MHz頻率聯繫彼此，創造出特有的傳訊文化。如今溝通工具已大幅減低人們傳訊的門檻，傳訊文化似乎並未隨之消逝，隱藏在我們已習以為常的溝通中。

Do you remember the days of the pager?

This 900MHz device let us stay connected to each other. The numbers on the screen not only conveyed simple messages, but also created a unique communication culture.Todays, staying in touch is easier, but no longer precious. We hope to bring you back to the 1990s, inviting you to relive the warmth of those numeric messages.

As the digits flash on the screen, you’re invited to experience the joy of hidden codes and feel the emotion behind each message once again.

浮海 ｜Pulsation Sea

< 2024 台南 月津港燈節-通往銀河的便利指南 >

< 台南 鹽水月之美術館典藏 >

此時，人們正經歷海平面每年上升3到4毫米的階段，隨著氣候變遷將遽增為每年4到9毫米。海平面的上升並非只是土地的消逝，更影響到人與環境的共處關係。《浮海》這件作品試圖放大人為干涉環境所造成的劇烈連鎖狀態，透過「蛇擺」曳引的視覺效果去重現人與自然時而「同步」時而「混沌」的牽制狀態，進而思考兩者間該如何共處，又該如何面對未來的異變。

Currently, sea levels rise annually at 3 to 4 millimeters, with predictions of an acceleration to 4 to 9 millimeters per year due to climate change. Beyond land loss, these rising sea levels significantly affect the human-environment relationship. “Pulsation Sea” magnifies the profound chain reactions resulting from human environmental interventions. Through a “Pendulum Wave” visual effect, it portrays the dynamic connection between humans and nature, oscillating between “synchronization” and “chaos”, stimulating reflection on coexistence and facing future uncertainties.
